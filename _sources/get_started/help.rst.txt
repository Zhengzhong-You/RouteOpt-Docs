Get Help
=====================================

If you have questions or need assistance with RouteOpt, please consider the following options:

- **Project Documentation & Q&A:**
  We recommend that you first check the project's documentation and Q&A section for detailed guidance and troubleshooting tips.

- **Community Chat & Support:**
  To receive consistent and ongoing updates, help, or advice—and to follow the newest directions of our research—we provide two main chat groups:

  1. **Slack (English):**
     If you wish to join our Slack group, please contact us via email at: ``you.z@ufl.edu``.

  2. **WeChat (中文):**
     A dedicated WeChat group is available for Chinese-speaking users, again, please contact us via email at: ``you.z@ufl.edu``.

- **GitHub Issues:**
  If you need further assistance or wish to report an issue, please feel free to open an issue on our GitHub repository.

We encourage you to explore these resources for the most up-to-date information and support.
